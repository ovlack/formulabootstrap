An ever-changing landscape

The 2022 Formula One World Championship is the 73rd season of the FIA Formula One World Championship, a motor racing championship for Formula One cars which is recognized by the sport's governing body, the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA). The season will consist of 22 Grands Prix, held in countries around the world from March to December 2022. The championship will see a number of significant changes, including the introduction of new technical regulations aimed at reducing downforce and increasing mechanical grip, and the expansion of the grid to 20 teams with the addition of new teams Alfa Romeo Racing Orlen and Andretti Autosport. The season will also see the return of the Dutch Grand Prix at Circuit Zandvoort, which last hosted a race in 1985. The defending World Drivers' Champion is Max Verstappen, driving for Red Bull Racing, while the defending World Constructors' Champion is Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One Team.
